FAQ

You can download Magma Maintained from the download page and replace your Magma jar with the new one.

What do I do if I already have Magma installed?

The Magma Foundation developers have retired and Magma Maintained will continue the development of Magma. This means they won't fix bugs or add new features anymore. Support here will be faster than ever and bugs will be fixed.

Why should I update to Magma Maintained?

Ketting, Mohist, Arclight and Crucible are other hybrids available.

What other hybrids are there?

You can report bugs on the GitHub page or on the Discord server. On Discord you can ask for help to the community and to the developers. Look at the rules before asking for support.

On Github you can open a new issue to make sure the issue will be fixed or at least seen by the developers.

What do I do if I find a bug?

You can add to your server Bukkit/Spigot plugins, Forge mods and datapacks. However, please note that some features may behave differently due to improvements and changes implemented in our software.
